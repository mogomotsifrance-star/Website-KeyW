What we offer

Comprehensive wellness, one partner

We group our work into three connected pillars — financial, workplace, and psychosocial & family wellness — so every intervention reinforces the others.

Financial Wellness Services

Practical coaching, education, and structured support that build lasting financial confidence and stability.

For organizations

Employee Financial Wellbeing Programs

Structured, organization-wide programs that help employees build healthier money habits and reduce financial stress at work.

One-on-one

Financial Coaching and Mentoring

Personalized coaching that turns financial goals into concrete, achievable plans, with ongoing accountability and support.

Workshops

Financial Literacy Training and Education

Practical, accessible training covering budgeting, saving, credit, and long-term planning for individuals and teams.

Individual support

Debt Management and Rehabilitation Support

Structured, judgment-free support for people working to understand, manage, and recover from debt.

Workplace Wellness Services

End-to-end programs that help organizations build healthier, more resilient, more supported teams.

Strategy

Corporate Wellness Solutions

End-to-end wellness strategy for organizations, designed around your culture and your people's real needs.

Confidential support

Employee Assistance Programs (EAP)

Confidential counselling and support services that help employees manage personal and work-related challenges.

On-site

Wellness Day Coordination and Health Promotion Campaigns

On-site wellness days and awareness campaigns that bring health resources and screenings directly to your team.

Transition support

Retrenchment Counselling and Transition Support

Compassionate, practical support for employees and organizations navigating retrenchment and career transition.

Psychosocial & Family Wellness

Counselling and tailored interventions that care for the emotional and social side of wellbeing.

Counselling

Psychosocial Support Services

Counselling and support for individuals navigating emotional, social, and psychological challenges.

Family-centered

Individual and Family Wellness Interventions

Tailored interventions that support wellbeing across the whole family system, not just one person.
